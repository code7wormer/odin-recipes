Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 3 ½ cups (437g) bread flour (plus extra for kneading)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (plus extra for the bowl)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes, oregano for garnish

Steps:

  1. Activate Yeast: In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. Mix Dough: Add the flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix with a wooden spoon or a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment until the dough starts to come together.
  3. Knead: Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5-8 minutes until it is smooth and elastic. Alternatively, use a stand mixer on low speed for 5 minutes.
  4. First Rise: Lightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in it, and turn it over to coat. Cover the bowl with a clean towel and let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat Oven: While the dough rises, preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone or steel, place it in the oven now.
  6. Shape Dough: Once risen, punch down the dough gently. Divide it in half for two medium pizzas. On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll one half into your desired pizza shape (about 12 inches in diameter).
  7. Add Toppings: Transfer the shaped dough to a parchment-lined or floured pizza peel/baking sheet. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your desired toppings.
  8. Bake: Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven, either onto the hot stone/steel or a baking sheet. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
