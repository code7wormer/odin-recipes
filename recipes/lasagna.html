Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat
  • Cheese(Ricotta,Mozzarella,Parmesan)
  • 1 Egg
  • Herbs of choice
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomatoes

Ingredients

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified by your recipe (usually around 375°F or 190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente, then drain and lay them flat on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a clean towel to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare your meat sauce (if using one) and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, half of the Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper until well mixed.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce across the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish to prevent the noodles from sticking.
  6. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce, overlapping slightly if necessary.
  7. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
  8. Spoon one-third of the remaining meat sauce over the ricotta mixture.
  9. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers twice more: noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella.
  11. Top the final layer of mozzarella with the remaining Parmesan cheese.
  12. Cover the baking dish tightly with aluminum foil.
  13. Bake the covered lasagna in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  15. Let the lasagna rest on a cooling rack for at least 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving to allow it to set properly.
